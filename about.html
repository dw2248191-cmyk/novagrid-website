Our Story

Why NovaGrid exists

NovaGrid Technologies was founded to bring U.S.-standard digital infrastructure practices — structured cabling, properly monitored networks, disciplined data center operations, and modern virtualization/cloud tooling — to markets that need them, starting with the Founder's home base of New York and New Jersey, and his prior professional market, Freetown, Sierra Leone.

NovaGrid is currently in its founding stage: building revenue through direct networking and infrastructure consulting services while laying the groundwork for a longer-term vision spanning data centers, cloud, WISP/ISP, security, and AI services. We'd rather tell you exactly where we are than oversell where we're going.

The Founder

Darrell Wilson

Data Center and Network Engineer with 8+ years of experience supporting enterprise Linux, virtualization, and data center infrastructure in large-scale environments. Also worked as a Hardware System Network Technician in Secaucus, New Jersey — racking, stacking, and troubleshooting server and fiber hardware. Previously spent nearly eight years as a network engineer in Freetown, Sierra Leone.
