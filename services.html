Available Now

Delivered directly, today

Network Design & Troubleshooting

Cisco routing/switching and MikroTik systems — design, installation, and troubleshooting for enterprise and small business networks.

Fiber Runs, Installation & Testing

Fiber runs, installation, splicing, and certified continuity/fault testing (OTDR, VFL) from a Certified Fiber Optic Technician (CFOT, CFOS/T, CFOS/S).

Server Imaging, Deployment & Administration

Server imaging and OS deployment, RHEL/Debian on bare-metal or virtualized infrastructure, backed by RHCSA certification.

Virtualization Support

VMware ESXi and Proxmox platform setup, migration, and ongoing administration.

Server Hardware Support

Dell and HP enterprise servers — RAID configuration, iDRAC, BIOS/firmware — plus GPU deployment and validation (NVIDIA A100/H100 experience).

Data Center Operations Consulting

Rack and cross-connect design, migrations, monitoring setup, and documentation — informed by direct operation of 230+ servers across 35+ racks.

Structured Cabling & Patching

Professional cable management, patch panel/cross-connect patching, and structured cabling installation for offices and server rooms.

Racking & Stacking

Server and network equipment installation into racks and cabinets, with clean cable management, for new builds and migrations.

Equipment Decommissioning

Safe removal and asset disposition of retired servers, network gear, and rack hardware.

Basic Cybersecurity Assessments

Practical, honestly-scoped security assessments for small and mid-size business environments.

General IT Consulting

Straightforward, technically-grounded IT consulting for businesses that need a second opinion or hands-on execution.

Electrical & Facility Cleaning Via Partner

Rack/facility electrical work (panel, conduit, and circuit-level power) and data center cleaning — coordinated end-to-end through licensed, vetted partners, not performed directly by NovaGrid staff.
